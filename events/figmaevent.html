🚀Get ready for 🎨

The Figma Hackathon!

(30th March, 2024)

Get ready to unleash your creativity and design prowess in our Figma Hackathon! Calling all students to dive into the world of Figma – the collaborative design platform reshaping the digital landscape.

What to expect from the event? Read below!

🌟 Introduction to Figma: We'll kick off with a comprehensive introduction to Figma, equipping you with the skills needed to bring your ideas to life. .

🌟 Live Practice Problem: Join us for a hands-on session as we tackle a practice problem together. Krithika will guide you through, ensuring you're ready to take on the challenges ahead.

🌟 Hackathon Challenge: Form a dynamic duo and dive into our week-long hackathon assignment. Unleash your creativity and innovation using Figma. The design challenge awaits! (Submission will be expected after 5 days of the event)

🌟 Team Collaboration: Collaborate with a partner and build a team of 2! Brainstorm ideas and bring your vision to reality. Team up, combine your skills, and conquer the design world together.

🌟 Win Exciting Prizes: The stakes are high! The winning team will be rewarded with a $100 cash prize, along with recognition for their outstanding design skills. It doesn’t end here. Winning team will also get an individual mentorship opportunity by Laxmi Peketi!
